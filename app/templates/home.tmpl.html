WaitStaff is a tool to compute meal costs and track tips over the course of a shift.
In the New Meal panel, input the cost of a meal, tax rate, and tip percentage the customer wants to leave. Computes the subtotal, tip amount and total amount to be charged to the customer.
Earnings panel displays cumulative information about all of the meals submitted. It shows the sum of all tips, Meal count, and average tip earned per meal.
